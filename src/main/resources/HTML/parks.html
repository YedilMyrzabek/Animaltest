Parks

Astana is home to several beautiful parks, offering plenty of green space and recreational activities for residents and visitors alike. Some of the most popular parks include Central Park and Astana Botanical Garden.

Nature Reserves

The city's nature reserves provide a peaceful retreat into untouched landscapes. The Korgalzhyn Nature Reserve is known for its biodiversity and is a must-visit spot for nature enthusiasts.

Recreational Areas

Astana offers many recreational areas for families and sports lovers. These include biking trails, picnic spots, and playgrounds scattered across the city.
