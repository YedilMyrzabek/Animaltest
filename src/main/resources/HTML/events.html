Festivals

Astana hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year. The Astana Music Festival is one of the largest events, attracting international musicians and performers.

Concerts

Live music events are popular in the city, with both local and international artists performing at venues like the Astana Opera House and the Palace of Peace and Reconciliation.

Exhibitions

Art lovers can enjoy exhibitions at the National Museum of Kazakhstan, showcasing local history, contemporary art, and more.
